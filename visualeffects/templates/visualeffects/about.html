Our VFX team develops high-quality visual effects for advertising agencies, videographers, film studios, individual filmmakers and many more businesses. Our VFX team can help organizations of all sizes - including entertainment/media companies. In addition to special effects and motion graphics for live action, Our team provides digital VFX services that include animation (2D and 3D), compositing, rotoscoping, matte paintings, green scene extraction and 3D modeling, 3D animation, 3D logo design, 3D FX, 3D tracking, 2D tracking . For example, rotoscoping entails a frame-by-frame tracing over live-action footage to achieve realistic animations. Matte paintings involve using a painted scene to produce an illusion of a different setting than the actual filming location.
